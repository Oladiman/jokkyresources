About Us

Jokky Resources

Jokky Resources company ltd was formed in 1999 in Port Harcourt, Nigeria by individuals with a passion for Engineering, Supply & Procurement, including Oil Tools Rental services. Our aim is to provide our customers with solutions for every kind, providing high quality, reliable services; highly motivated. Customer focused personnel; meticulous project Engineering/job design and an innovative approach to product development and technological solutions.
We are committed to providing a superior level of customer service which is sometimes found lacking in today’s largely profit driven organizations.
Whatever challenges you face Jokky Resources would relish the opportunity to offer a cost effective technology/solution that meets your needs. Jokky Resources can help you to avoid repeating past mistakes by putting it right.
